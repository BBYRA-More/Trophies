O.G. Dale Memorial Trophy

Outstanding Sportsmanship in BBYRA

O.G. Dale Memorial Trophy

“Awarded for a period of one year in recognition of Outstanding Sportsmanship in the Barnegat Bay Yacht Racing Association.”

This award recognises outstanding sportsmanship and conduct. Any sailor, skipper or crew, senior or junior, is eligible to be nominated. “If no one’s name readily comes to mind during the racing season, there is no need to make this award.”

The trophy was donated by F. Slade Dale in June of 1936, in memory of his father — Orton G. Dale. The original bronze cup has had three silver bands and bases added over the years, the most recent in 2005, to make room for the winners’ names.

George Roth Powell, Jr. Trophy

Outstanding Junior — Character & American Sportsmanship

George Roth Powell, Jr. Trophy

In memory of George Roth Powell, Jr. — Annual Award to the Outstanding Junior of Barnegat Bay for Character and American Sportsmanship.

This trophy is awarded annually to the junior sailor on Barnegat Bay who best demonstrates outstanding character and sportsmanship throughout the racing season. Any junior sailor, skipper or crew, is eligible to be nominated by any member club of the BBYRA.

B.B.Y.R.A. Race Management Trophy

Excellence in Regatta Planning, Execution & Conduct

B.B.Y.R.A. Race Management Trophy

This trophy is awarded to promote, further, and improve the sport of yacht racing by encouraging proficiency in the planning, execution, and conduct of yacht racing events by member clubs of the BBYRA. The trophy was donated by Past Commodore Roger Brown.

Administrative Requirements:
  • Minimum 10 yachts entered (5 for US Sailing round-robin events)
  • At least three closed course races
  • Non-professional race management
  • Minimum 60% completed skipper ballots returned
  • Results, Notice of Race, and Sailing Instructions submitted to BBYRA no later than 6 days before end-of-season awards

George Nelson Memorial Past Commodores Trophy

Most Participation in BBYRA Championship Series

George Nelson Memorial Past Commodores Trophy

Awarded annually to the member yacht club fostering the most participation in yacht racing on Barnegat Bay. Presented in honour of the past commodores by the family of George M. Nelson, Commodore 1962–63.

Criteria: The award is based on the number of “Qualifiers” from each member club in the BBYRA Championship Series. In the event of a tie, the number of registered boats from each club will be used to break the tie.

Leonard Paul Egee Memorial Trophy

Greatest Junior Participation in BBYRA Championship Series

Leonard Paul Egee Memorial Trophy

The Leonard Paul Egee Memorial Trophy is presented to the active member club fostering the greatest participation of juniors in yacht racing on Barnegat Bay.

The award is based on the number of junior “Qualifiers” from each active member in the BBYRA Championship Series. In the event of a tie, the number of boats registered by juniors from each active member will be used to break the tie.

In the event the trophy is not awarded for two consecutive years, ownership of the trophy reverts to the donor if living, or the donor’s heirs if the donor is deceased. The original trophy was replaced by a new trophy in 2011.
